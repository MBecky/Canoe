Welcome to Rugby Canoe Club

We try to make it a friendly club welcoming families and paddlers of any age. As you develop your skills the club offer BC awards in recognition of your ability.

Most types of canoeing are offered from General Recreational and Touring, Slalom, Flat Water, Surf, Sea, Freestyle and White Water. The club also caters for stand up paddle boarding.

Membership

We are an open club with 80 plus members and it is our aim for you to safely experience the exciting world of paddle sport under the supervision of the British Canoeing (BC) qualified coaches.
